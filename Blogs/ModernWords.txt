English language is the most universal language in history. To enjoy and learn from
what you read you must understand the meaning of the words a writer uses.
Vocabulary is a matter of word-building as well as word-using and only few
activities are as delightful as learning new vocabulary. Now not everything can be
taught in the classroom – that’s why I’ve put together a list of 10 newer English
words and figures of speech for you to sprinkle into your English conversations:
Earworm: A song or tune that is stuck in your head. Ex: That new Selena Gomez
song is such an earworm!
