The structure of the course is designed in a way that students have to study the core courses from different functional areas of management that are compulsory. Later on, specializations are offered in functional areas where the students can opt for two specializations out of the five offered. Marketing, Finance , IT, HR and International Business and one Open Specialization. Right from the beginning of the course, the focus is on providing relevant inputs through case discussion/ analysis, simulation games, role plays etc. keeping in mind the current business scenario.
